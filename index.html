About Us

Melbur Studios is a one-stop shop for all software
and graphic-related services.

I have been in the information, communication and technology field since around 2011. In that time, I have done a Bachelor's Degree in Law school and a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. However, one challenge or another has made it hard for me to finish these courses with the spirit I started them with. In June 2023, I joined ALX with some expertise in software engineering and a year later, I feel as enthusiastic to complete the course as I started it. The greatest inspiration to run this project is because over the years, I have realised that there is a severe shortage of professional websites where a service provider such as myself can offer their services to persons located all over the world. This platform will offer graphic design, web development, software engineering, photo and video editing, and product branding services among many others. It does not matter where you are located in the world, give a brief description of what you want and we will deliver a quality product for you and your business
